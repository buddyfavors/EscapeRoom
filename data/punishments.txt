# KnottyBytes Escape Room — Wheel of Punishments
# One punishment per line. Blank lines and lines starting with '#' are ignored.
#
# Special prefixes:
#   minigame             → the engine picks a random minigame
#   minigame:<slug>      → forces a specific minigame (reaction-rush | whack-mole | rps | simon | pattern)
#   text:<message>       → explicit text punishment (same as any un-prefixed line)
#
# Anything else is shown as a text punishment on the player display.
