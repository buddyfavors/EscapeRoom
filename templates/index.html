Escape Room

Pick a difficulty and launch the run. Each valid RFID scan (10 digits) rolls good or bad — good reveals one character on a random unsolved lock (~40% of scans come up bad on Medium). Shorter digit strings try the numeric locks, five letters try a letter lock. Three bad codes in a row — wrong lock combinations or bad RFID rolls — spin the wheel of punishments. A good RFID clue or opening a lock resets the bad streak. Every three good RFID rolls (bad rolls do not count) forces a minigame.
